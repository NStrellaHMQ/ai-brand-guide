## VISUAL STYLE RULES
- **Shapes:** Use rounded rectangles with medium corner radius 
- **Lines:** Clean, geometric lines. No messy hand-drawn effects.
- Border: 1–2px neutral gray stroke
- Elevation: Flat (no shadow)
- **Iconography:** Use "Outlined" style icons with a consistent stroke weight. Fill the interior of the icons with **#FFC000**. 
- **Diagrams:** Spacing should be open and uncluttered to maintain "Clarity."
- **Texture:** Use flat design principles. Avoid 3D skeuomorphism, heavy drop shadows, or photographic textures.
